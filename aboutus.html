The Nabila Laksa and Laksam Berkuah story began with the opening of our 1st restaurant at Kg Kangkong, Pasir Mas, Kelantan. We currently serve the best Laksa and Laksam in Kelantan made with fresh fish to make the gravy or "kuah" and premium flour for the laksa. As the no.1 Laksa and Laksam in Kangkong, we are constantly working to better serve our customers with great moments, excellent services with the best Laksa and Laksam version of us.

Not only that, we have two types of gravy which are "Lemak" and "Pineng" where the “Lemak” gravy uses a recipe from Kelantan and a recipe from Pineng. This makes customers love to get Laksa and Laksam at our store.
